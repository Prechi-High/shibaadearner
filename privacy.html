Privacy and Security

Last Updated: August 08, 2025

At RewardApp, we prioritize your privacy and security. This Privacy and Security Policy explains how we protect your data and ensure a safe experience on our Telegram Mini App ("App").

1. Data Security

1.1 Secure Storage: All your account details, including user ID, balance, referral statistics, earning statistics, and check-in data, are securely stored on our servers. Only you can access your personal information.

1.2 No Unauthorized Access: We use robust security measures to prevent anyone, including other users, from accessing your balance or any other account details.

2. Phone Number Protection

2.1 Safe Verification: The phone number you link to our App is 100% secure. We never share it with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

2.2 Limited Use: Your phone number is used solely for account verification and App functionality, ensuring your privacy is maintained.

3. No Third-Party Data Sharing

3.1 User Data Protection: We do not share your personal data (e.g., user ID, balance, referral stats, earning stats) with third-party advertisers, including those sponsoring tasks in our App.

3.2 Restricted Information: Your IP address, bank details, online activity, and location are never shared with third parties. We are committed to keeping this information private.
